Hurricane Hilary

Southern California Updates

Weather

Key Facts

  • Category: Category 2
  • Projected Path: Heading towards the west coast of the United States
  • Wind Speed: Maximum sustained winds of 110 mph
  • Rainfall: Expected heavy rainfall, potential for flooding

Preparations and Safety

Residents in the affected areas are advised to:

  • Stay informed through official weather channels
  • Follow evacuation orders if given
  • Secure outdoor items to prevent damage
  • Stock up on essential supplies, including water and non-perishable food

Emergency Services

Local emergency services are on standby to provide assistance and information. Shelters have been set up for those who need a safe place to stay.

Aftermath and Recovery

After the storm passes, communities will assess the damage and initiate recovery efforts. Power outages and road closures are expected, so residents are urged to stay safe and patient.
