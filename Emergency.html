Emergency Links and Numbers for Hurricanes

Emergency Links and Numbers for Hurricanes

Emergency Services

Dial 911 for immediate assistance in any emergency situation, including hurricanes.

Number: 911

National Hurricane Center (NHC)

The NHC provides up-to-date information about hurricanes, including forecasts, watches, and warnings.

NHC Website

Number: (305) 229-4470

Local Authorities

Stay informed by following the instructions and updates provided by local emergency management agencies, as well as state and local government websites.

American Red Cross

The American Red Cross provides assistance during disasters, including hurricanes, by offering shelter, food, and support to affected individuals and communities.

American Red Cross Website

FEMA (Federal Emergency Management Agency)

FEMA is responsible for coordinating the federal government's response to disasters. They provide valuable resources and information for hurricane preparedness and recovery.

FEMA Website

NOAA Weather Radio

NOAA Weather Radio broadcasts continuous weather information, including updates on hurricanes, for your specific area.

NOAA Weather Radio Frequencies

Local News Outlets

Stay tuned to local news channels on television, radio, and online platforms for updates, advisories, and evacuation notices related to hurricanes.

Mobile Apps and Alerts

Many smartphone apps offer weather alerts and emergency notifications.

Make sure to download a reliable weather app and enable emergency alerts for your area.

Community Notification Systems

Some regions have community notification systems that send alerts to your phone about emergency situations, including hurricanes.

Check with local authorities to see if this service is available in your area.
