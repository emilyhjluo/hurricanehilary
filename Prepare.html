Hurricane Preparedness

Preparations for Hurricanes

Stay Informed:

  • Monitor weather forecasts and updates from reputable sources like the National Hurricane Center (NHC) or local meteorological agencies.
  • Sign up for emergency alerts and notifications from local authorities.

Create an Emergency Kit:

  • Non-perishable food (canned goods, energy bars, etc.)
  • Drinking water (at least one gallon per person per day for several days)
  • Manual can opener
  • Medications and a first aid kit
  • Personal hygiene items
  • Flashlights and extra batteries
  • Portable phone charger or power bank
  • Cash (ATMs may not be functional during power outages)
  • Important documents (ID, insurance policies, medical records) in a waterproof container

Develop a Family Emergency Plan:

  • Establish meeting points in case family members are separated.
  • Assign responsibilities for various tasks (evacuation, pet care, etc.).
  • Ensure all family members understand the plan.

Stay Safe During the Storm:

  • Stay indoors away from windows during the storm.
  • Avoid using candles, and opt for battery-powered lights to reduce the risk of fires.
